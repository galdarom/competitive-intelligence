Who leads — WAF

Imperva and Radware lead on WAF depth, both pairing a positive-security (behavioral/ML auto-policy) model with negative signatures and offering true hybrid on-prem plus cloud deployment that the pure-cloud vendors cannot match; Imperva carries the strongest analyst pedigree (repeated Gartner MQ Leader, Forrester Wave Leader). Cloudflare and Akamai deliver excellent edge-native WAFs with massive managed-ruleset automation, but only protect traffic that traverses their networks. Radware's edge differentiator is auto-generated granular policies with low false positives plus DAST-driven virtual patching.

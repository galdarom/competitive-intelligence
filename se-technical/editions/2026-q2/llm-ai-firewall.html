Who leads — LLM / AI Firewall

LLM/AI firewall is the youngest and least mature pillar, and GA status varies sharply by vendor. Cloudflare leads on maturity: its AI Security for Apps (formerly Firewall for AI) reached general availability in March 2026 with prompt injection and PII detection. Imperva AI Application Security and Radware LLM Firewall both launched in November-December 2025 as model-agnostic reverse-proxy guardrails - Imperva's datasheet still carries a beta label. Akamai Firewall for AI was announced in April 2025 but remains in limited availability. SE teams should treat this market as fast-moving and verify current GA status per deal.

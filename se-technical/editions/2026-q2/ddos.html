Who leads — DDoS

Cloudflare leads on raw network scale (388+ Tbps across 330+ cities, every PoP an always-on anycast scrubbing center), while Akamai Prolexic leads on dedicated scrubbing and SLA strength (20+ Tbps, 32+ scrubbing centers, an industry-leading zero-second time-to-mitigate SLA). Radware's differentiator is detection accuracy and genuine hybrid defense: DefensePro on-prem appliances with patented behavioral algorithms hand off to a ~15 Tbps cloud scrubbing network for real-time, low-latency mitigation - the strongest fit for carriers and latency-sensitive enterprises. Imperva offers solid multi-terabit cloud scrubbing with a 3-second SLA but a smaller network and no on-prem appliance line.
